What is a Hackthown

The hackathon aims to bring together creative minds to solve challenges related to technology,
social good, and innovation. Participants will create projects in teams and compete for prizes.

How It Works

Build Your Project: Once the hackathon begins, you and your team will have a set amount of time (usually 24-48 hours) to work
on your project. The theme or challenge will be revealed during the kickoff, and you'll need to create a solution that aligns with it.
Whether its an app, website, or prototype, youll use your skills and creativity to bring your idea to life. Throughout the event,
you can use various tools and resources, and you'll have access to mentors who can help guide you through technical challenges.

Judges

Our panel of expert judges includes industry leaders from top tech companies. Theyll evaluate projects based on creativity, impact, and technical excellence

Mentorship

Throughout the hackathon, experienced mentors will be available to guide you, offer feedback, and help you overcome technical challenges. Get the support you need to bring your project to life!
