Seraph of the End

My favorite manga is Seraph of the End (終わりのセラフ), written by Takaya Kagami and illustrated by Yamato Yamamoto. It's a thrilling story about vampires, survival, and friendships.

Here's why I love this manga:

- It has an epic story filled with action and emotions.

- The characters are deep and well-developed.

- The art style is absolutely stunning!

Some of my favorite characters are:

- Yuichiro Hyakuya

- Mikaela Hyakuya

- Shinoa Hiragi

If you're a fan of dark fantasy and action, you should definitely read this manga!
